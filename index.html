Bachelor in Applied Computer Technology


The BACT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development and network systems administration. Further more, it prepares learner with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derrived from be BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31 series of 2021.

Program Objective:


The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development, and network/ systems administration, and supervisorial skilss to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a start up business of puruse a teaching career.

Specific objectives:

The learner should be able to:

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the importsnce of being an ethical computing profrssional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network system, and development of productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/ service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
  5. Make positive contributons to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the Program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and pofessional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities.
